Recruit U

Giving Students a Head Start

About the project

Recruit U is an iPhone app designed to help students connect to College athletic programs based on a wide range qualifications. The app gives students and coaches direct contact to one another.

My role

I was hired to design the look and feel or Recruit U.Working closely with the developers, we aimed to build a simple app, on par with Apple's IOS 7 human interface guidelines.

Hitting the Mark

The recruit U mark was designed to symbolize stability and strength, with the student and university at the center.

The App

Making it’s way into multiple news articles and reviews, the app functions beautifully. Both for the ambitious athlete and talent hungry coach.

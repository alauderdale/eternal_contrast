About the project

Wellhire is a innovative piece of software with simple offerings. The company makes hiring a breeze through online assessments. I was hired by wellhire to design a new look and feel for the product.

My Role

I worked with wellhire to completly re-design and re-build their product. We aimed to preserve popular functionality and highlight new features through a shiny new interface system.

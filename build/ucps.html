One School To Rule Them All

Giving back to my high school.

About the project

We were hired by Union Colony Preparatory School to completely re-imagine their website. Their existing site had been outdated for some time, and they were seeking a fresh perspective on their web presence.

My Role

I worked with UCPS to build a systematic web design approach aimed to best serve the students and parents. I delivered a fresh website with an emphasis on content and school info.

The Road Less Traveled

The school recently opened a new location to support more students. We designed the site to allow easy access to both schools, with simple sidebar navigation to support links to individual school. Each school had it's own presence, incorporating Union Colony's school mission.

Long Term Solutions.

We outfitted the new site with a custom Wordpress back end, allowing admins and teachers to easily update content on the site, including events, teacher bios and news.

View the Site

Connecting Parents & Teachers

Custom CMS

We built the new site with a custom Wordpress back end, allowing admins and teachers to easily update the content on the site. Including events, teacher bios and news.

Event Manager

We developed a custom event management system so parents and students could stay up to date with school functions and sporting events.

Teacher Bios

Every teacher was given their own bio page complete with ability to post assignments and accept feedback.

Featured Content

We developed a featured content slider, so that the school could easily display updates and important alerts.
